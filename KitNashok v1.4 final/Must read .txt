Install:
First extract the zip file.
Now execute(double-click) Setup.exe from the extracted folder. give the admin permission if it wants. 
Now, press 'C' if you want to customize or press any other key to go with default settings.
When installation is done, it want's to scan your whole system. press 'y'.

Uninstall:
launch KitNashok icon from desktop  and press
[tab] >[tab] >[1]


or you can just execute uninstaller.exe and press 1
